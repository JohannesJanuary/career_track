Operations analytics project focused on issue patterns, delay causes, customer impact, and process improvement.

Target rolesOperations Analytics, Business Analyst, Data Analyst
ToolsPython, SQL, Excel, Power BI
DatasetOrders, back orders, supplier delays, issue categories, customer contacts
Business questionWhat patterns drive back-order delays and service bottlenecks?
Final assetsNotebook, dashboard, root-cause summary, improvement memo
Resume valueUses prior back-order reduction and customer-service experience

Build Requirements

AnalysisFind top delay causes, cycle-time patterns, and high-impact customer segments.
