Major Certification 1 | June-July 2026

Microsoft Certified: Power BI Data Analyst Associate (PL-300)

Primary certification for BI analyst, data analyst, sales analytics, operations analytics, and reporting analyst roles.

ProviderMicrosoft Learn / Pearson VUE
Estimated U.S. costAbout $165; Microsoft says price varies by proctoring country or region.
Duration8 weeks, 5-7 study hours per week
Skill levelIntermediate
Tools neededPower BI Desktop, Power Query, DAX, Excel, SQL basics, sample business datasets
Exam focusPrepare data, model data, visualize/analyze data, manage and secure Power BI

Why This Matters

PL-300 should be the first major certification because it directly supports dashboard development, KPI reporting, business requirements translation, data modeling, and stakeholder-ready visual storytelling.

Weekly Calendar

Week 1Review official exam guide, install Power BI Desktop, map strengths and weak areas.
Week 2Power Query, cleaning, joins, data types, and repeatable data-prep workflow.
Week 3Star schema, relationships, calculated columns, measures, and model design.
Week 4DAX fundamentals, KPI measures, time intelligence, and dashboard calculations.
Week 5Visual design, slicers, drill-through, tooltips, filters, and accessibility.
Week 6Build the sales dashboard portfolio project and write the KPI dictionary.
Week 7Practice assessment, weak-area review, and rebuild one dashboard section from memory.
Week 8Final practice, exam scheduling, portfolio polish, and LinkedIn credential update.

Portfolio Deliverable

Sales Performance and Revenue Dashboard: Power BI dashboard, SQL cleaning script, KPI dictionary, screenshots, project page, and one-page executive recommendation memo.
