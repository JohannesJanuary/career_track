Salesforce Tableau Certified Data Analyst

Visual analytics credential for Data Analyst, BI Analyst, Sales Analytics, and Operations Analytics roles.

ProviderSalesforce Tableau
Official exam cost$250 exam fee
Duration8 weeks, 5-7 study hours per week
Skill levelIntermediate; Tableau recommends meaningful product experience
Tools neededTableau Public or Desktop, Excel/CSV datasets, SQL basics, calculated fields, dashboard design practice
Exam focusBusiness questions, data exploration, visual analytics, dashboards, calculations, and actionable insights

Why This Matters

Tableau complements Power BI and shows that Johannes can operate across common BI environments. It is especially useful for employers that use Tableau for visual analytics and executive reporting.

Weekly Calendar

Week 1Review Tableau exam guide, install Tableau Public/Desktop, and choose project dataset.
Week 2Connections, joins, relationships, cleaning assumptions, and exploratory analysis.
Week 3Calculated fields, table calculations, parameters, filters, and dashboard controls.
Week 4Dashboard design, actions, tooltips, navigation, and stakeholder usability.
Week 5Insight writing, visual storytelling, and business recommendation structure.
Week 6Build retail operations or sales territory Tableau portfolio project.
Week 7Practice exam, weak-area rebuilds, and timed dashboard tasks.
Week 8Final review, schedule exam, publish Tableau project, and update portfolio.

Portfolio Deliverable

Retail Operations KPI Dashboard or Sales Territory Performance Dashboard: interactive Tableau dashboard, project page, metric definitions, screenshots, and business recommendations.
