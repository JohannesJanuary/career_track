PMI Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)

Project-management credential for Business Analyst, Project Analyst, Operations Analyst, and Project Coordinator roles.

ProviderProject Management Institute (PMI)
Official exam cost$225 PMI member / $300 non-member
Duration8 weeks, 5-7 study hours per week
Skill levelFoundational project management
Tools neededPMI CAPM prep materials, project charter template, stakeholder map, risk log, process map
Eligibility noteConfirm 23 hours of project-management education before applying.
